The cost of a modular home depends on its size, floor plan, materials, features, and delivery location. Smaller modular and tiny homes are generally more affordable, while larger custom homes cost more. Contact Martin Boxabl for current availability and pricing information.

In many cases, portable homes can be more affordable than traditional site-built houses because they are manufactured in controlled factory environments that reduce construction time and material waste. However, final costs vary depending on transportation, installation, and site preparation.
