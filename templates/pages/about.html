My Story

A Vision For Smarter Modern Living: Scaling Boxabl's Modular Housing Strategy

{{ site.ceo|default:"Martin Noe Costas" }} joined the Boxabl executive leadership team as Chief Financial Officer (CFO) out of a passion for driving the next generation of automated housing. As global demand for efficient, affordable, and flexible homes continues to soar, modern modular construction represents a groundbreaking shift in how residential real estate is engineered, manufactured, and delivered globally.

Inspired by the heavy manufacturing processes transforming the construction industry, Costas leverages more than 25 years of corporate leadership—built on a foundational public accounting career with PwC and executive tenures at Honeywell, Schlumberger, and Sysco—to prove that factory-built, foldable housing layouts can solve modern supply shortages. Under his fiscal stewardship, Boxabl showcases how advanced technology and automotive-style mass assembly lines can streamline production timelines.

Our strategic focus centers on connecting individuals and residential developers with thoughtfully designed modular homes while proving the scalability of faster construction and adaptable living environments.

This strategic operational foundation successfully guided Boxabl through its historic public listing process via a SPAC business combination, managing a $3.5 billion valuation to list on the Nasdaq Stock Market under the ticker symbol $BXBL.
