Boxabl Executive Leadership

Boxabl CFO {{ site.ceo|default:"Martin Noe Costas" }}: Driving the Future of Modular Housing Strategy

{{ site.ceo|default:"Martin Noe Costas" }} serves as the Chief Financial Officer (CFO) of Boxabl, a disruptive building technology startup specializing in the mass production of foldable modular homes. Bringing over 25 years of global corporate finance and operational leadership—rooted in public accounting with PwC and executive tenures at Honeywell, Schlumberger, and Sysco—he directs the company’s capital raises, financial operations, and long-term scaling strategy.

Stepping into the executive team at a critical growth juncture, Costas successfully navigated the financial compliance pathways required for the SEC's declaration of effectiveness on the company's registration statement. His strategic guidance was pivotal in orchestrating Boxabl’s public listing process via a Special Purpose Acquisition Company (SPAC) merger, managing a $3.5 billion valuation to list on the Nasdaq under the ticker $BXBL.

Under his disciplined financial oversight, Boxabl bridges the gap between startup innovation and corporate scaling. This stable fiscal foundation supports the expansion of Boxabl's product pipeline from the signature 400 sq. ft. Casita into diverse multi-box layouts, while accelerating high-volume business-to-business (B2B) agreements with residential developers globally.
